Loka Grinding Guide

Sand

To collect sand, head to Garama docks and then proceed north. Bring 2-3 shovels and shulker boxes.
Exchange rate: 2-3 shulker boxes for 1 shovel.
(Diamond Armor is essential if you lack neutral mobs buff).

Note: Always be prepared for potential hostile mobs in the area.

Nether wart can be found in various spots around the Nether. Use a Fortune 3 tool for maximum yield.
Coordinates provided below. Alternatively, explore the Nether and harvest until no more is found.

You should have about or more than 2 shulkers when finishing this run.

Glowstone

To collect glowstone, bring blocks and an elytra. Pillar up and break the glowstone on top of Nether caves.
Note: The old patches of glowstone underground no longer work!

Infested Territories

Infested Territories are filled with constantly spawning monsters. Custom loot tables replace standard mob drops.
Drops include: gunpowder, ghast tears, blaze powder, glowstone dust, slime balls, exp bottles, etc.
There are two types of Infested Territories:

  • Yellow Wilderness - Gear retained on death, but Unstable Loot dropped.
  • Red Wilderness - Typical wilderness rules apply, but tougher mobs yield more loot.

Infested Territories rotate every 2 weeks, starting on Truce drop, then every 2 weeks after. Check the world map for infested tiles - yellow or red: LokaMC Map.

Bring: health pots, strength, DBR, Ancient ingots for armor repair, shulkers for storage, and a bow may prove useful.
For protection, build a hut inside the infested tile using blocks and a door to secure your shulkers and buff up away from monsters.
Alternatively, buff up with strength, speed, and DBR for survival.

Resource Isle

Mining on Loka is a quick way to obtain money and diamonds. Each continent (Garama, Ascalon, Kalros) has three resource isles (RIs), each with different ore spreads.

  • Garama boasts the most condensed spread, making it ideal for quick ore collection.
  • Ascalon offers a large amount of ores spread over a vast area.
  • Kalros features abundant ores but is challenging to navigate due to ice and lava.

Coordinates for Resource Isle Caves:
Kalros RI Cave: 3178, 5132
Garama RI Cave: 3347, 1208
Ascalon RI Cave: 7099, 4853
2530, 5390 (for Kalros, "funny cave")

WARNING: Resource Isles are prime PvP spots. Exercise caution to avoid losing your items upon death. Ensure nobody follows you.

Kit for Resource Isle:
Invisibility is just in case you need to run away (pearling in lava and drinking is normally the best way to hide).
1 Shulker should be empty to store ores and the other should be full of pots.
Ender Chest should be full of pots.
Pickaxe needs to be silk touch for storage purposes (use Fortune 3 once you are back to your town safe).
Elytra is for getting to/from Resource Isle. If Elytras are disabled on that continent, you need to use a boat instead.

Obsidian

To gather Obsidian, head to the Dragon Isle and locate the tallest tower. Simply mine downwards from the top to collect Obsidian efficiently.
How to get to Dragon Island:
Go to the end docks and then to the coordinates: x = -983, z = 329.
Use an Ender Pearl to enter the End Gateway.

Food

Grinding Food:

For grinding food, head to Ascalon docks where cows spawn in groups of 2-4. Make sure to bring a Looting 3 sword and appropriate armor for efficient farming.

Recommended Grinding Areas:

  • Ascalon: Birch forest (near docks), Sunset Isle
  • Garama: Savanna
  • Kalros: Plains and spruce forest (near docks)

Additionally, for wheat, visit Ascalon forest. Potatoes can be obtained from Garama, while carrots can be found in Kalros. Alternatively, you can grow potatoes and carrots in your town for a sustainable food source.

Ruins

What are The Ruins?

Ruins are old, fallen towns converted into ruins by admins. In ruins, you can find diamond armor, tools, brewing materials, and most importantly, armor trims. Every container has a chance to contain loot (e.g., furnaces, chests), so leave nothing unlooted. Additionally, loot pouches can be found around ruins which contain loot. When you're near them, you can hear a sparkling sound.

  • Ascalon: Seraphim - 3834 / 4756, Valinor - 4100 / 6500, Heldenstadt - 3425 / 2525, Thunderhold - 5400 / 1800
  • Garama: Amberfell - 5218 / 3300, Varmastrand - 3300 / 2700, New Arxem - 1800 / 4000, Vantis - 1200 / 2900, Camelot - 2100 / 5100
  • Kalros: Nerd Nation - 1200 / 3000, ??? - 5700 / 4200, Karldrsvell - 5800 / 2400, Seagulls - 1200 / 2338
Money Making

Money Making Methods

Here are some methods to make money in the game:

Good Money Making Methods

  • Red infested tiles - 24-30k shards per hour
  • Ri Mining - 10-16k shards in approximately 30 minutes to 1 hour
  • Netherwart farming - 10-30k shards per hour depending on the farm

Decent Money Making Methods (average profit per hour)

  • Shrines - 8-10k shards per hour
  • Ender Pearl farming (dragon isle) - 7-12k shards per hour
  • Elytra farming - 1k every 5-10 minutes (7-14k shards per hour)
  • Mining obsidian - 9k shards per hour
  • Wood (Spruce/Dark oak) - 8-12k shards per hour
  • Yellow infested tiles - 10-18k shards per hour
  • Wheat, Potatoes, Carrots - 5-7k shards per hour (bonemeal-based farm)

Bad Money Making Methods (don't try)

  • Farming food (cows, pigs, sheep) - 5-9k shards per hour (spawn rate is bad, buying food or industry is a better option)
  • Glowstone - 5-8k shards per hour
  • Dreadfort farming - 5-9k shards per hour (blaze rods, magma cream) only good if double drops

Some Other Money Making Methods

  • Wither skull farming - 30-50% chance to drop wither skull every 22 minutes
  • Shulker box farming - respawns every 3 hours (can get 20-40 shulker shells) (double drops)
  • Sticky webbing - Just don't
  • Market flipping - luck-based 5-30k shards per hour
  • Buying from players (for cheap) and reselling on market (high price)
  • Buying sets worth less than diamond price and relisting (NPC diamond prices)

Most items don't sell fast, only pot mats.

Town Leveling

Town Leveling Methods

Hey, basically this is how to level a town from level 1 to level 22-25 within a week or two (Depending on how many people help out/how much you no-life).

Mining

Go to the Resource Isle (Garama and Kalros mostly) and just mine it out, ores give the most exp when it comes to this town level so it's a sure bet to level up your town.

Logging

This depends on what alliance you're in and where your town is located.

If you control the Mega Taiga, (And your town is on kalros / your in a kalros alliance which has biome control) your able to plant a bunch of 2x2 spruce trees and just chop until you can't do it anymore.

If you control the Ancient Jungle (Or maybe even jungle, not too sure) your able to do the exact same as what i said above, but with jungle trees.

If your in NEITHER of these places, my best bet is to either chop on the top of your tile or go to Garama Docks and chop the wood there. OR head to the Mushroom Isle on Ascalon (2148, 7608) and chop down the mushroom trees for EXP as well. (shoutout monkeynick for telling me this)

Excavation

This also depends where your town is located, but not so much.

If you are in the Clay Cliffs region of Ascalon (and you have biome control), your able to obtain clay and get a bunch of excavation levels. You also can smelt the clay for crafting levels, which is nice.

If you aren't in the Clay Cliffs, you have two options. Either dig out all of the soul sand in the nether, which gives more EXP but soul sand is genuinely useless, or dig out all of Garama Beach (4457, 1897) or parts of the Garama Desert (2496, 2822) to get a bunch of sand, which gives you a little less EXP but it's genuinely more useful for building or brewing.

Farming

Go to the Ascalon Wheat Fields (1796, 4500) and just break all the wheat you can find. Will level up the farming level pretty fast.

Enchanting

Spam enchant level one enchants (with books) until you hit Enchanting Level 40, then spam enchant books until you hit around Level 60 to 70. Not worth going past it unless you have a surplus of XP bottles and books.

Alchemy

Honestly just brew. Build Moccs (Moccs link) and shit out pots until you run out of mats.

Blacksmithing

Craft diamond sets and tools. Enchant these to get enchanting levels too, a bit of a 2 in 1 level strat

WorldPvP

Go to conquest fights and get kills, or go ganking and kill people. Only way to level up this level is to stab people

Building

Build your town. Place blocks, any block doesn't matter just by building your war room or your town schematic, also, wood slabs gives you a lot of crafting levels

Crafting

Smelt clay balls, it sounds weird but it gives ALOT of town levels. It's crazy asf

Ganks

What is Ganking?

Going to Zyre Cove/Fissure and battling other players in 1v1s or "ganks" (Fights consisting of more than 1 player on each side). Also my favorite part of Loka.

When you die at Cove/Fissure, all items in your inventory and /b are lost including shards.

Maps

Normal Zyre

  • Large map, never rotates.
  • Corruption on certain parts of map.
  • Diamond armor/tools/weapons become unstable on entry.
  • Escape by waiting on the airship which changes locations every 10 minutes.
  • Unpopular, not many fights.

Zyre Cove/Fissure

  • Small maps, rotates every few days (/cal)
  • Almost all water sources corrupted.
  • Items not unstable on entry.
  • Escape by using /kill at spawn while NOT in combat.
  • Your items do not become unstable upon entering.
  • Popular, near constant fighting.

Firestone

What is the Firestone? The Firestone is an item that gives you infinite fire resistance.

The nether area is located at x-121, z-249 at spawn. Watch the video for guidance.

  • Tips:
    • Don't bring shulkers because you can't place them.
    • Bring 1-2 stacks of food as the parkour is difficult and you may spend a while there.
    • Bring pearls, but note that they don't work in the temple area.
    • Carry instas and keep them in your hotbar in case of an unexpected encounter.
    • Bring at least 2 Fire Resistance potions.
    • To leave, use /kill (if you're not in combat). Alternatively, you can walk out and pearl to the boat.

TO CLAIM: Once you complete all 3 tasks, the Firestone will show up in your SoulBound bag. To open it, use /b and click on the ender chest.
